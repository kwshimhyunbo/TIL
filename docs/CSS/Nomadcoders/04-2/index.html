Green apple pie

CAL190
CHO17g
CAP11g
CFF8g
INTRODUCE

For pastry, mix flour and salt; cut in shortening. Add water 1 tbsp at a time, using only enough to make a workable paste (too little will make it crumbly). This pastry, being exceedingly rich, must be handled deftly. Roll out pastry and line pan. Make 2 (9 inch) shells. Thorough chilling before rolling makes pastry easier to handle. For filling, line pie pan with one pastry shell and fill with sliced apples.
